Our platform

Technical direction for teammates, teachers, and contributors—kept separate from pricing and our team story.

Architecture & stack (planned)

The public web client is planned in Angular. Server-side services are planned in Java, with Domain-Driven Design (DDD) and a Clean Architecture-style layout so domain rules (homes, devices, consumption) stay testable as alerts, exports, and billing grow.

Angular - Frontend

Java - Backend

  • Angular — structured UI, reusable components, and room to grow into a full dashboard experience.
  • Java + DDD — domain-focused modules instead of scattering logic across layers.
  • Clean boundaries — clear separation between UI-facing use cases, core domain, and infrastructure (data stores, integrations).

Pricing

Plans that match how you use your smart home

TechWatch follows a freemium model: start free to explore centralised monitoring and basic energy visibility, then upgrade when you need deeper history, smarter alerts, and exports for serious optimisation. All amounts below are in Peruvian soles (PEN), indicative—they may change with taxes, IGV, or launch promotions.

Free

For renters and first-time smart-home owners who want clarity without commitment.

S/ 0 / mes

  • Up to 5 registered devices across your home
  • Room-based device organisation (living room, bedroom, etc.)
  • Essential dashboards — live status and recent activity at a glance
  • Basic energy view — see consumption trends for the last 7 days
  • Mobile-friendly web UI — control-centre style access from your phone
  • Community email support (best-effort response times)

Premium

For households that want history, proactive alerts, and data they can act on every month.

S/ 15 / mes cobro mensual · cancela cuando quieras

  • Everything in Free, plus a higher or unlimited device cap (as defined at launch)
  • Extended metrics history — compare weeks and months, spot seasonal patterns
  • Custom alerts — thresholds for usage spikes, offline devices, or unusual schedules
  • Export & reports — CSV/PDF-friendly summaries for budgeting and audits
  • Priority support — faster responses as we scale the team
  • Early access to new analytics and integrations as they ship

TechWatch is under active development—features listed here reflect the intended roadmap. Early releases may use simulated data until device integrations are live.
