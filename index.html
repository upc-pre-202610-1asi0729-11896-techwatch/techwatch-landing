Pricing

Plans that match how you use your smart home

Three paid tiers in PEN (indicative). TechWatch is in active development, so features will roll out gradually and early versions may use simulated data until integrations are live.

Starter

Entry monitoring for a small set of devices and rooms.

S/ 10 / mes

  • Core device management views
  • Live status for a limited device count
  • Room grouping for essentials
  • 7-day activity snapshot
  • Email support (community tier)

Standard

More history, alerts, and exports for active households.

S/ 15 / mes

  • Everything in Starter, with a higher device cap
  • Extended history for trends and comparisons
  • Custom alerts for usage and offline events
  • CSV-friendly exports for budgeting
  • Faster support queue

Complete

Full visibility, reporting, and priority access as we scale.

S/ 20 / mes

  • Everything in Standard, top device tier at launch
  • Long-range analytics and seasonal views
  • Reports suitable for audits and optimisation
  • Priority support and roadmap previews
  • Early access to integrations as they ship
