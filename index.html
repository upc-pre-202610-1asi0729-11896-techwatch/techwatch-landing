Pricing

Plans that match how you use your smart home

Freemium: use it for free and upgrade for advanced features like history, alerts, and exports. Prices are in PEN. TechWatch is in active development, so features will roll out gradually and early versions may use simulated data until integrations are live.

Free

For renters and first-time smart-home owners who want clarity without commitment.

S/ 0 / mes

  • Up to 5 registered devices across your home
  • Room-based device organisation (living room, bedroom, etc.)
  • Essential dashboards — live status and recent activity at a glance
  • Basic energy view — see consumption trends for the last 7 days
  • Mobile-friendly web UI — control-centre style access from your phone
  • Community email support (best-effort response times)

Premium

For households that want history, proactive alerts, and data they can act on every month.

S/ 15 / mes

  • Everything in Free, plus a higher or unlimited device cap (as defined at launch)
  • Extended metrics history — compare weeks and months, spot seasonal patterns
  • Custom alerts — thresholds for usage spikes, offline devices, or unusual schedules
  • Export & reports — CSV/PDF-friendly summaries for budgeting and audits
  • Priority support — faster responses as we scale the team
  • Early access to new analytics and integrations as they ship
